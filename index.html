Data Science Reimagined with Xperiflow

Launch your business into the future of AI and ML technology with transparent, easy-to-implement solutions. Our Xperiflow engine was designed with your business problems in mind. Build and deploy powerful, tailored, and ML-enabled models in minutes.

What is the Xperiflow ML engine?

At DataSense, our experts have developed a state of the art machine learning engine capable of handling a wide range of common business forecasting and clustering problems. With our Xperiflow-powered application, business users will have the ability to walk step by step alongside their data through our ML pipeline, going from data to deployed model within minutes.

How can my business trust the Xperiflow results?

We recognize that transparency and adaptability are two of the biggest hurdles in machine learning and its applications. To eliminate this risk, we have made our engine almost see through by nature. You can see exactly how and when different data science techniques are being applied through our endpoint infrastructure. We also supply common univariate baselines against which Xperiflow's results can be compared, providing you as the user the assurance that you are getting the most out of your data.

What makes Xperiflow better than our current modeling?

One of the biggest perks of machine learning and specifically Xperiflow is its use of multivariate datasets to identify patterns. This means that a user is able to provide additional features, such as events, weather, and customer influx, which will be used and optimized to provide insight to your predictions.

Does this require a large team?

By keeping our endpoints simple, logic driven, and easy to interact with, we have been able to abstract out large amounts of the complexity surrounding machine learning. Xperiflow-enabled applications can provide a user friendly wizard-based approach to model building, walking through the entire process in a way that any business user can understand and act on. The helps us reduce the team necessary to “do” data science down to almost zero!
