Data Science Reimagined with XperiFlow

Launch your business into the future of AI and ML technology with transparent, easy-to-implement solutions. Our Xperiflow engine was designed with your business problems in mind.

What is the XperiFlow Engine?

At DataSense, our experts have developed a state-of-the-art machine learning engine capable of solving a wide range of business problems. XperiFlow blends automation, cutting edge data scientist intuition, and a systematized model development workflow to deliver highly performant machine learning solutions in a fraction of the standard time and cost.

Why was XperiFlow created?

The current machine learning development landscape remains largely disjointed. There are an unmanageable number of frameworks, systems, and infrastructure that a business needs to fit together just to get one solution deployed. Pair this high level of technical complexity with the need for specialized personnel to build, manage, and deploy the solutions and the probability of a successful data science initiative is dramatically reduced. XperiFlow was specifically designed to counteract this disjointed infrastructural landscape by automating, and systematizing the machine learning pipeline building process for all data science problems.

How can my business trust the XperiFlow results?

DataSense recognizes that transparency and interpretability are two of the biggest hurdles in machine learning and its applications. With XperiFlow, businesses can see exactly how and when different data science techniques are being applied throughout the model building process. XperiFlow also features model health monitoring, automatic model retrains when model performance drops, and model spinoff statistics ranging from feature importance to performance metrics. Baseline models that mimic standard planning approaches are automatically generated and compared against the XperiFlow models in order to ensure that DataSense is providing value to your business.

Does this require a large team?

XperiFlow-enabled applications provide a wizard-based approach to model building. DataSense packages the data science expertise, system architecture, and an understandable UI experience all under one roof to allow users to focus only on solving the problem on hand. Let XperiFlow provide the trusted machine learning pipeline infrastructure so model building teams can remain slim, agile, and responsive to the business problem.
